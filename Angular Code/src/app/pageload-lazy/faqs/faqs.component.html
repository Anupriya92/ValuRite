  • ValuRite is a mobile-cum-web based solution that works for Lenders, Property Valuation companies and individual property valuers/ appraisers. They can use this product for Home Loan valuations, Income Tax valuations & NCLT cases.

    Dashboard:
    Every user is provided with the dashboard with the status of their valuations.

    Workflow:
    You can create your own list of field executives / appraisers, assign & monitor the status of the valuation for the list of valuations. You can instantly view the report once the field executive captures the data at the site and validate them before they leave the site. This will help in the TAT for your appraisal process. With comprehensive data capture forms, you get reliable and accurate valuations with simplified approval workflow processes. It is user friendly, hosted on the cloud and accessible on-the-go, anytime, anywhere. It has the ability to capture the pictures and integration with Google maps for capturing the latitude and longitude and nearby places.

    Scheduling site visits:
    Your field executives would be able to schedule / reschedule the site visits with borrowers. They can provide the list of documents required for verification when they visit the site when scheduling the appointment.

    Invoicing:
    You can capture the invoice amount for each appraisal and raise the invoice to the lender. You can also capture the payments received and get an ageing report for the outstanding invoices to enable you to follow-up.
